Meet Our Staff

Dedicated, passionate, and united in our mission to protect Canada’s nature and wildlife.

Our Team

The Nature Canada team is composed of biologists, campaigners, communicators, and conservationists who work together to make lasting change for nature. Each team member brings expertise and passion to our collective goal—defending the natural world we love.

Executive Director

Provides leadership and strategic direction to Nature Canada’s programs and partnerships across the country.

Conservation Manager

Leads science-based initiatives and conservation programs that protect habitats and species at risk.

Communications Lead

Drives outreach, storytelling, and advocacy campaigns that elevate the voice of nature across Canada.

Community Engagement Officer

Connects with local communities, Indigenous partners, and volunteers to build grassroots impact.
